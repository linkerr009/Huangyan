About HUANGYAN SOURCING

Connecting overseas buyers with all-category product resources, coordinated suppliers and practical cross-border supply chain support from Huangyan, Taizhou and across China.

Who We Are

A China Sourcing Platform Built on Public-Sector Confidence

Taizhou Huangyan District Trade Group Co., Ltd. was established in 2024 and operates HUANGYAN SOURCING as a state-owned backed cross-border trade and supply chain platform.

Supported by government resources and regional industry networks, we bring together product categories, supplier resources, physical showrooms and export coordination services. Our role is to help overseas buyers identify suitable products, communicate with suppliers and move sourcing projects forward with clearer information and stronger local support.
